The term “Six Sigma” was originated by a Motorola engineer - Bill Smith and Six Sigma is a federally registered trademark of Motorola. He defined that Six Sigma is a practice which involves various systematic and rigorous tools for getting close to “Zero Defects”.

Six Sigma is a data driven methodology which is applied with an objective of eliminating the waste and inefficiency and achieving total customer satisfaction.  Lesser defects leads to yield improvement and higher yields means enhanced customer experience; this further leads to less cost of operation.

Deploying Six Sigma within an organization is a big footstep and involves many activities. One has to plan a route, define objective, stick to what is feasible, preparing leaders, creating Six Sigma organization, training the organization and working
