Shinelearning.com is an e-learning marketplace brand being launched by Shine.com with a vision to up-skill the Indian talent pool to adapt to the changing job market. We're the largest marketplace (aggregator) for e-learning courses in India with 500+ courses by both Indian, government-recognized certification providers as well as global leaders in skilling courses.

Shine Learning uses an algorithm that not only predicts emerging skill sets but also recommends courses to its users after analyzing their resume and application history. Shine Learning strives to bring the best courses from vendors across the globe, completely, unbiased. It’s one of a kind platform that equips users with skills and helps them find a job.

Our courses are designed in line with hiring trends and have high acceptance in the market. These certifications come with flexible study options; giving an edge to professionals who can work and learn at the same time.
