Dr Ajay Garg

Associate Professor – Digital Marketing XLRI Jamshedpur

Dr. Ajay Garg is a full-time professor of information Management. Professor Garg’s primary expertise and interest is in the area of business analytics including text mining, data mining, aftificial intelligence, machine learning, predictive analytics, big data and various techniques for knowledge discovery in databases.

Ajay graduated from the XLRI, in 1997, with a Post Graduate Diploma in Communications and a specialisation in Media Planning & Media Marketing. During the course of his career, he has worked with leading global agencies like Mediacom, Lintas Media Group, Universal McCann, OMD and Dentsu. He has also worked with leading global market research agencies like ACNielsen & TNS Global.

Over course of his career, Ajay has had the opportunity to manage the traditional as well as digital media mandates for clients across categories viz. Toyota, Lexus, Hitachi, Toshiba, McDonald’s, Fonterra, Carlsberg, Ikea, Guinness, Etisalat Telecom, Mitsubhishi Motors, Bajaj Auto, Haier Electronics, Videocon, Sansui, Standard Chartered Bank, Stanbic Bank (SA), Arab National Bank, Tata AIG Insurance, SBI Mutual Funds, Big Bazaar, McDonald’s, Reckitt Benckiser, Beiersdorf, Nestle CPW, GroupeDanone, Parle Biscuits, Britannia, Wrigley’s, GlaxoSmithKline, Pfizer.

In 2013, he setup the 1-year specialization course in Digital Communications Management at MICA, which is offered as a part of the 2-years Post Graduate program.
